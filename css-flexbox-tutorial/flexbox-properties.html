Session - 3: CSS Flexbox and Responsiveness

CSS Flexbox Properties

  • Properties for the parent i.e., Flex container: 
    • display: flex | inline-flex
    • flex-direction: row | row-reverse | column | column-reverse
    • flex-wrap: wrap | nowrap | wrap-reverse
    • Shorthand of flex-direction and wrap: 
      flex-flow: [direction] [wrap]
    • justify-content: flex-start | flex-end | center | space-between | space-around | space-evenly | ..
    • align-items: flex-start | flex-end | baseline | stretch | center
    • align-content: flex-start | flex-end | stretch | baseline | center | space-around | space-between
  • Properties for the Children i.e., Flex items: 
    • flex-basis: [value] | auto
    • flex-grow: [unitless_value]
      *no negative numbers 
    • flex-shrink: [unitless_value]
    • To define order of render without changing the HTML source structure: 
      order: [value]
    • Shorthand: 
      flex: [flex-grow | 0] [flex-shrink | 1]? [flex-basis | auto]?
    • align-self: flex-start | flex-end | center | baseline | stretch
